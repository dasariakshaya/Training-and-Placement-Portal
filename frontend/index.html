Our Recruitment Process

1. Company Registration

Recruiters express interest and register on our portal, creating their company profile.

2. Job Notification (JNF)

Companies submit a Job Notification Form with role details, eligibility, and salary.

3. Pre-Placement Talks

Companies present to students, sharing insights about their work culture and opportunities.

4. Selection Process

Online tests, technical interviews, and HR rounds are conducted to select the best candidates.
